Founder & CEO

Meet Cypril Udeh

Born in 1992 in the heart of Lagos State, Cypril's journey began with a deep love for sports and an eye for fashion. After graduating with honors in Corporate Management Economics from Nnamdi Azikiwe University (UNIZIK), he combined his academic knowledge with his entrepreneurial spirit.

In 2015, with just a small kiosk in Mandillas Market and a vision to redefine sports fashion, Cypril laid the foundation for what would become Sports Jersey. His unique approach blended premium quality with streetwear aesthetics, creating jerseys that resonated with both athletes and fashion enthusiasts.

"We don't just sell jerseys - we craft confidence. Every stitch tells a story of Nigerian excellence."

Today, Cypril leads a team of passionate designers and sports enthusiasts, constantly pushing boundaries to deliver innovative designs that celebrate both athletic performance and street style.
